About Project Sobeys

YawHide is a multi platform application.

The goal of Project Sobeys is to ultimately let its users save money by being able to sort and easily find the best deals in their local grocery flyers.

Project Sobeys only supports Sobeys for now, but in the very near future, it will support a lot more grocery stores. Canada only as well!
